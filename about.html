Our Story

A legacy of passion for horological excellence

The EternalElegance Heritage

Founded in 1995 by master watchmaker Henri Laurent, EternalElegance began as a small atelier in Geneva dedicated to preserving the art of traditional watchmaking.

What started as a passion project to restore vintage timepieces evolved into a global destination for collectors seeking the rarest and most exquisite watches.

Today, under the leadership of Henri's daughter Sophie Laurent, we continue this legacy while embracing innovation in horology.

Our Values

Craftsmanship

We celebrate the artistry and precision of traditional watchmaking, selecting only pieces that meet our exacting standards.

Authenticity

Every timepiece undergoes rigorous authentication by our team of experts before joining our collection.

Legacy

We believe luxury watches are heirlooms meant to be cherished across generations, not just accessories.
