Masterful Chronograph

The Chronograph Master represents the pinnacle of our limited edition collection, blending traditional watchmaking techniques with contemporary design. Each component is meticulously finished by hand, from the guilloché dial to the hand-beveled bridges of the movement.

The 18k rose gold case measures 42mm in diameter and features both polished and brushed finishes, creating a striking play of light. The sapphire crystal case back offers a view of the beautifully decorated movement, including the column wheel chronograph mechanism.

Dial Details

Silver guilloché dial with applied rose gold indices, small seconds at 9 o'clock, 30-minute counter at 3 o'clock, and tachymeter scale on the outer rim.

Movement

Manual-winding chronograph movement with 72-hour power reserve, 21,600 vibrations per hour, and 23 jewels. Certified chronometer.
